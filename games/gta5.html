GTA V

  • Developer: Rockstar Games
  • Release Year: 2013
  • Genre: Action, Open World

About: GTA V follows three main characters — Michael, Franklin, and Trevor — in the chaotic city of Los Santos. The game offers a vast world with heists, missions, and an online mode (GTA Online) that adds years of replayability.

Fun Facts: 1. One of the best-selling video games of all time (over 180 million copies). 2. Includes first-person and third-person views. 3. Modding community is huge, especially on PC.
