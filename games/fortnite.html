Fortnite

  • Developer: Battle Royale, Survival, Creative
  • Release Year: 2017
  • Genre: Epic Games

About: Fortnite is a free-to-play multiplayer game that combines building mechanics with fast-paced combat. It offers several game modes: Battle Royale, Zero Build, Creative Mode, and Save the World. The game is updated regularly with events, crossovers (like Marvel, Star Wars, and anime), and limited-time modes.

Fun Facts: 1. Over 500 million registered players worldwide. 2. Hosted in-game concerts with artists like Travis Scott and Ariana Grande. 3. Frequently features collaborations with pop culture icons and brands. 4. “Zero Build” mode was added due to high demand for more casual combat.
